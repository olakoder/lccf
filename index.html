Why Choose Us

OUR MISSION

Enriching lives through our optimized social Welfare Enterprise

OUR VISION

Exploring the Pool of Available Public and Private Resources for the benefit of the citizens

OUR OBJECTIVES

SELF RELIANCE TRAININGS

To organize skilled oriented and ready for work trainings that ensure self reliance for the youths, elderly ones and equally for professionals to maximise productivity in their respective careers.

The training also to build resillient leaders that will stand for self, community and the nation at large.

SUSTAINABLE BALANCE

To address economy, social, enviromental and cultural diffrences in a way to build the society, we are all craving for.

COLLABORATIVE APPROACHES

To create a strong alliance with the organizations of the same objectives, build a strong relationship with various communities within the country and in diaspora as well as Local, State, Federal Goverment of Nigeria and those outside the country with the purpose of lifting up the standard of citizens.

CHARITY AND COMMUNITY WELLBEING WATCH

To work within the frame of Govenment policies by biding for the Government of all levels contracts, projects, programmes and carry them out to use the profits purposeful to ameliorate the standard living of the people. Also to act as charity organisation to different companies, industries and organisations to receive grants from them. Raise funds from wealthy individuals to ease the hardship of citizens created by uneven distribution of the resources, striving for better standards and voices of the communities.
